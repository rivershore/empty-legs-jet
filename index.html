Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly are empty leg flights?

Empty leg flights occur when a private jet needs to return to its home base or reposition for its next charter without passengers. Instead of flying empty, operators offer these flights at significantly reduced rates.

How much can I save with empty leg flights?

Typically 50-75% off regular charter prices. A flight that normally costs $50,000 might be available for $12,500-$25,000 as an empty leg.

Are empty leg flights safe?

Absolutely. They use the same aircraft, crews, and safety standards as regular charter flights. The aircraft must meet all FAA (or equivalent) safety regulations.

Can I choose my departure time?

Empty leg flights have fixed schedules since they're repositioning flights. However, with 90+ flights available at any time, you can often find one that fits your schedule.

How do I book an empty leg flight?

Simply click "Book Now" on any flight that matches your needs. You'll be redirected to our partner Villiers who handles all bookings securely.

What happens if my flight gets canceled?

Operators guarantee empty leg bookings. In the rare event of cancellation, you'll receive a full refund or assistance finding an alternative flight.
